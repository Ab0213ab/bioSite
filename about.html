About

Jude seems to have always had a creative mind; she went to school for art and design and was once the Director of the Design Department for the May Company, a massive upscale department store chain operator. Jude was an interior designer for a Midwestern design firm and a co-owner of a design firm and furniture store in Steamboat Springs, Colorado. Jude also started and operated a food business in Washington State and has sold residential real estate in both Florida and in Washington. These career accomplishments are impressive, but when asked about her proudest achievements and goals, Jude mentioned her family multiple times. Being there for her loved ones has always been a major goal for Jude and the love she has for everyone in her family is something she is rightfully proud of.

Jude Schaefer’s life story is one of creativity, selflessness, vigor, and inspiration. Jude had a quiet and morally conscious small-town upbringing in Emporia, Kansas, where when she was born the census bureau estimated the town only had a population of just over 13,000 people. Her healthy childhood allowed her to develop a strong moral compass that eventually led to her political involvement and interest in community service and social justice issues. Jude is justifiably proud of some of the work she has done over the years; she volunteered for former President Barack Obama's campaign, as well as marched in the first Woman's March in downtown Seattle. Jude has given her time to the Head Start Program (link to external site here) which is an organization run by the Department of Health and Human Services that aims to assist families in need by providing early childhood education, health, nutrition, and parent involvement services. Jude’s favorite quote by Mahatma Gandhi perfectly sums up her outlook on life: “You must be the change you wish to see in the world.”

As someone who has always been full of energy, it comes as no surprise that Jude Schaefer she has kept that vivacity into her golden years as she still sells real estate, hikes, travels, exercises, sails, and snorkels. Many positive things have occurred in Jude’s life, but her story also includes immense heartbreak; Jude lost her first husband to drugs, her son to suicide, two parents and two brothers have passed, and recently Jude lost her daughter to brain cancer. However, through these “hailstorms” as she calls them, Jude has never lost her passion for others or her enthusiasm for life. Jude’s ability to continue to bring about positivity even in the worst of times makes her a genuine inspiration.
